Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Qawama account and all associated data using either of the following methods:

  • From within the app: Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • By sending an email request to qawama.app@gmail.com

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase authentication record
  • All cloud backup data stored on Supabase

All local data on your device (transactions, budgets, categories) is also wiped when you uninstall the app. Nothing is retained.

Delete Your Data Without Deleting Your Account

If you want to clear your data but keep your account, you can do so from within the app:

  • Settings > Data > Clear All Data — deletes all local transactions, budgets, and categories from your device
  • Settings > Backup > Delete Backup — removes your encrypted cloud backup from Supabase

Data Retention

Qawama does not retain any of your data after a deletion request. All deletion is immediate and permanent. There is no recovery period or grace window.

Contact

For any data deletion requests or questions, email us at qawama.app@gmail.com
