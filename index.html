Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your Data Stays Yours

Qawama stores all your financial data locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your transactions, budgets, or any personal financial information.

What We Access

  • SMS messages — only bank transaction SMS, read on-device, never sent anywhere
  • Internet — only for exchange rates (ExchangeRate-API), Firebase authentication, and optional encrypted backup
  • Storage — to save your data locally and import bank statements

Cloud Backup

Cloud backup is completely optional. If you choose to enable it, your data is encrypted on your device with AES-256 encryption using a passphrase only you know. The encrypted data is stored on Supabase. We cannot read your backup — only you can decrypt it with your passphrase.

No Ads, No Tracking

Qawama has no advertisements, no analytics trackers, and no third-party SDKs that collect your data. We don't sell, share, or monetize your information in any way.
